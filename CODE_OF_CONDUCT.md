# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Public or private misconduct
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.
